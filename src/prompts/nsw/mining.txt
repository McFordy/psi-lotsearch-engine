## Section: Historical Mining Activity (New South Wales)

Generate the mining activity section for a NSW PSI report.

### Interpretation Rules

Mining Databases:
- Historical Mining Shafts
- Mining Titles and Leases
- Petroleum Exploration Licences (PELs)

Historical Mining Shafts:
- Report any shafts identified within the buffer: location, distance/direction, shaft type
- Mining shafts within 100m are significant — potential for ground instability and contamination

Petroleum Exploration Licences (PELs):
- Critical assessment: historic PELs often cover very large areas (hundreds of square kilometres) for regional exploration. These are typically dismissed as: "The PEL relates to regional petroleum exploration activities covering a broad geographical area and is not considered to represent a site-specific contamination source relevant to the Site."
- Only flag PELs as significant if they relate to specific wellhead locations within the buffer

### Output Format

Create subsections for mining activity and petroleum exploration.
