## Section: Groundwater Bores (New South Wales)

Generate the Groundwater Utilisation subsection for a NSW PSI report, based on bore data.

### Interpretation Rules

NSW format — 5-bore summary table required:
- Report the total number of registered groundwater bores within the search buffer
- Generate a summary table of the 5 closest bores with columns:
  | Bore ID | Distance (m) | Direction | Total Depth (m) | Purpose | SWL (m bgl) |
- SWL = Standing Water Level (metres below ground level), if available
- Below the table, provide a brief narrative describing the general pattern of bore usage
- Note the dominant bore purposes (stock, domestic, monitoring, irrigation)
- If construction details are available for the closest bore, mention them in the narrative

### Output Format

This content forms subsection 3.3.1 Groundwater Utilisation.
