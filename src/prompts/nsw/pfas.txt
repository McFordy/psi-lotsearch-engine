## Section: PFAS Investigation & Management Programs (New South Wales)

Generate the PFAS section for a NSW PSI report. This covers all PFAS-related databases.

### Interpretation Rules

Databases covered:
- EPA PFAS Site Investigations
- Defence PFAS Investigation & Management Program — Investigation Sites
- Defence PFAS Investigation & Management Program — Management Sites
- Airservices Australia National PFAS Management Program

For nil results across all programs: combine into a single statement noting that no PFAS investigation or management sites were identified within the search buffers.

For hits: report each site with name, address, distance/direction, and current status. PFAS sites within 2km are significant.

### Output Format

Single section covering all PFAS programs. Group nil results together.
