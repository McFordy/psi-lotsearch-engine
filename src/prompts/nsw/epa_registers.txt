## Section: EPA NSW Regulatory Registers

Generate the EPA regulatory register review subsections for a NSW PSI report. This covers EPA NSW contaminated land and pollution databases.

### Interpretation Rules

Key NSW registers:
- CLM Act Contaminated Sites (s.58 Contaminated Land Management Act 1997): sites currently regulated under the CLM Act. Report the management class and current status.
- Records of Notice: s.58 CLM Act management orders, voluntary management proposals, and ongoing maintenance orders.

For each register, report:
- Whether records were identified on-site, within 100m, and within the buffer
- For nil results, use the standard nil statement
- For hits, describe each record: address, distance/direction from Site, nature of the listing, management class, and your assessment of contamination risk

Distance-based risk assessment:
- On-site (0m): Always flag for review — high significance
- Less than 100m: Significant — assess based on activity type and hydraulic gradient if known
- 100-500m: Moderate — assess based on activity type
- Greater than 500m: Low — typically dismissed unless high-risk activity

### Output Format

Create a subsection for each register. Group nil-result registers together where appropriate.
