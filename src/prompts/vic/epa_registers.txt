## Section: EPA Victoria Regulatory Registers

Generate the EPA regulatory register review subsections for a Victorian PSI report. This covers all EPA Victoria databases searched.

### Interpretation Rules

For each register, report:
- Whether records were identified on-site, within 100m, and within the buffer
- For nil results, use the standard nil statement
- For hits, describe each record: address, distance/direction from Site, nature of the listing, and your assessment of whether it represents a material contamination risk

Distance-based risk assessment:
- On-site (0m): Always flag for review — high significance
- Less than 100m: Significant — assess based on activity type and hydraulic gradient if known
- 100-500m: Moderate — assess based on activity type, typically dismiss low-risk activities
- Greater than 500m: Low — typically dismissed unless high-risk activity (landfill, gasworks, heavy industry)

Key VIC registers:
- EPA Priority Sites Register (current contaminated sites under active management)
- EPA Site Management Orders
- Former EPA Priority Sites (historically listed, since removed — note this means remediation likely occurred)
- Contaminated Land Notifications (s.53 EP Act — notifications, not confirmed contamination)
- EPA Permissions (scheduled activity registrations — categories like A23 waste storage, A13c waste recovery, D07 dairy processing)
- Legacy EPA Licensed Activities (former EPA licences — report licence type, activity, address, distance)
- Legacy EPA Works Approvals (former works approvals)

For Environmental Audits and GQRUZ: Do NOT include these in this section — they have their own dedicated prompt.

### Output Format

Create a subsection for each register group. Group nil-result registers together where appropriate.
