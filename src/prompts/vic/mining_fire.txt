## Section: Historical Mining Activity & Natural Hazards (Victoria)

Generate the mining activity and natural hazards sections for a Victorian PSI report.

### Interpretation Rules

Historical Mining Activity:
- Historical Mining Shafts (from Earth Resources Database)
- Report any shafts identified within the buffer: location, distance/direction, shaft type
- Mining shafts within 100m are significant — potential for ground instability and contamination from historical mining operations
- For nil results, use standard nil statement

Fire History:
- Bushfire Prone Area designation
- Fire History records
- Report whether the Site is within a designated Bushfire Prone Area
- Note any recorded fire events within the buffer

### Output Format

Create separate subsections for mining and natural hazards.
