## Section: Waste Management Facilities, Landfills & Liquid Fuel Facilities (Victoria)

Generate the waste management and liquid fuel facilities section for a Victorian PSI report.

### Interpretation Rules

Waste Management Facilities:
- National Waste Management Facilities Database
- Statewide Waste and Resource Recovery Infrastructure Plan Facilities
- Legacy EPA Prescribed Industrial Waste records
- EPA Victorian Landfill Register
- Former Waste Disposal Sites (historical landfills from council records)

For each hit, report: facility name/owner, address, distance/direction, facility type, status (active/closed).
Landfills within 500m are significant — flag for review regarding potential landfill gas and leachate migration.
Former waste disposal sites: note the fill type, prior use, and current use.

Liquid Fuel Facilities:
- Former Gasworks: historically significant contamination sources (coal tar, PAHs, heavy metals). Flag any within 1km.
- National Liquid Fuel Facilities: service stations, fuel depots, terminals. Assess based on distance and type.

### Output Format

Create subsections for waste management and liquid fuel facilities separately.
