## Section: Historical Business Directory Records (Victoria)

Generate the Historical Business Directory section for a Victorian PSI report.

### Interpretation Rules

- Distinguish premise-match (0m, on-site) from road-match (same road) and area-match (general area)
- Premise matches are the most significant — these indicate the business was likely at or very near the Site
- Flag potentially contaminating activities (PCAs): dry cleaners, service stations, motor garages, chemical manufacturers, metal works, tanneries, printers, photographic studios, paint/varnish manufacturers
- For non-contaminating businesses (grocers, drapers, newsagents, bakers), note their presence but state they are not considered to represent a contamination risk
- Group similar entries together (e.g. all service stations, all dry cleaners)
- Report the date range of directory records searched
- For road and area matches beyond the immediate Site, note the distance and assess relevance

### Output Format

Summarise findings by category:
1. On-site or premise-match PCAs (most significant)
2. Nearby PCAs within 100m
3. PCAs within the buffer but beyond 100m
4. Summary statement about non-contaminating businesses
