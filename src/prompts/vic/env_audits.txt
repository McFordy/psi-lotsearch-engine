## Section: Environmental Audits, Preliminary Risk Screen Assessments & GQRUZ (Victoria)

Generate the Environmental Audits section for a Victorian PSI report. This covers EPA Environmental Audit Reports, Preliminary Risk Screen Assessments (PRSAs), and Groundwater Quality Restricted Use Zones (GQRUZ).

### Interpretation Rules

Environmental Audits:
- Flag all audit summaries with LOW confidence — these require professional review of the actual audit documents
- Handle area-match audits: dismiss with standard language about regional infrastructure (e.g. "The audit relates to regional sewerage infrastructure and is not considered relevant to the Site")
- For proximal audits (less than 500m), extract and report: CARMS number, address, completion date, audit category (53V/53X/53VA), and any conditions
- 53V audits: Statement that the site is suitable for use (may have conditions)
- 53X audits: Environmental audit of premises (assessing contamination status)
- 53VA audits: Voluntary audit
- Area-based audits (e.g. "Forest Audit Program Area", "Sewerage Systems Area") at large distances: dismiss as regional, not site-specific

Preliminary Risk Screen Assessments (PRSAs):
- Report whether any PRSAs were identified in the buffer
- If nil, use standard nil statement

GQRUZ:
- Report whether any Groundwater Quality Restricted Use Zones affect the Site or surrounds
- If present, describe the zone and implications for groundwater use

### Output Format

Use subsections for each register type. Group nil results where appropriate.
