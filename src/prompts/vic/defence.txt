## Section: Defence Sites & Unexploded Ordnance (Victoria)

Generate the defence sites section for a Victorian PSI report.

### Interpretation Rules

Databases covered:
- Defence Controlled Areas
- Defence 3 Year Regional Contamination Investigation Program
- National Unexploded Ordnance (UXO) register

For nil results across all databases: combine into a single statement.

For hits:
- Defence Controlled Areas: note the area name, distance, and relevance to contamination risk
- UXO sites: flag for review — any UXO designation within the buffer requires professional assessment
- Regional contamination investigation sites: note the investigation status and relevance

### Output Format

Single section covering all defence-related databases.
